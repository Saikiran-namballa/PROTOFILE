      • Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on a checkered gameboard with 64 squares arranged in an 8×8 grid. The game is played between two players, one controlling the white pieces and the other controlling the black pieces. The goal of the game is to checkmate the opponent's king, which means placing the king in a position where it cannot escape capture (a "check") and has no legal moves (a "mate").
